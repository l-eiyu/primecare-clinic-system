🔒 PrimeCare Medical Clinic is committed to protecting your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information — Full name, date of birth, contact number, email address, and home address
  • Medical Information — Blood type, allergies, reason for visit, doctor's notes, prescriptions, and diagnosis
  • Account Information — Username, encrypted password, and account activity
  • Payment Information — Transaction records (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Usage Data — Pages visited, features used, and appointment history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage your appointment bookings
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates via SMS and email
  • Maintain your medical records and appointment history
  • Process payments for consultations and services
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Clinic Doctors and Staff — To provide medical care and manage appointments
  • Payment Processors — Stripe, GCash, and Maya for secure payment processing
  • SMS Providers — Twilio for appointment reminders
  • Cloud Services — AWS for secure data storage and hosting
  • Legal Authorities — When required by law or court order

4. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • AWS Certificate Manager for SSL/TLS certificates
  • AWS IAM for strict access control
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive medical information
  • Regular security audits and monitoring via AWS CloudWatch

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal and medical data for as long as your account is active and as required by Philippine medical record-keeping regulations. Upon account deletion, your data will be anonymized or deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173), you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, readable format
  • Object — Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@primecare.ph

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. We use:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the website to function properly
  • Session Cookies — To keep you logged in during your session
  • Analytics Cookies — To understand how users interact with our platform

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. Cloud Storage (AWS)

PrimeCare uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) to host and store data securely. Specifically:

  • Amazon S3 — Stores website files and patient documents
  • AWS CloudFront — Delivers content securely and efficiently
  • AWS IAM — Controls who can access your data
  • AWS CloudWatch — Monitors system health and security events
  • AWS SES — Sends transactional emails securely

All data stored on AWS is encrypted at rest and in transit.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18 without parental consent. If a parent or guardian is booking on behalf of a minor, they are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of the information provided.
